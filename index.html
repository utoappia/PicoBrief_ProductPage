PicoBrief

YouTube video summaries you can read, listen to, and quiz yourself on.

What PicoBrief Does

🎧

Audio Summaries

Listen to a 2-minute audio brief of any YouTube video. Perfect for commutes and workouts.

📝

Detailed Notes

Full markdown summaries with key points, timestamps, and tables. Export to PDF.

🧠

Interactive Quizzes

Test your understanding with auto-generated quiz questions and explanations.

🌍

12 Languages

Summaries generated in 12 languages with native text-to-speech voices.

🔍

Topic Discovery

Browse summaries by topic. Create custom topics to find videos that match your interests.

🆓

Completely Free to View

All summaries, audio, and quizzes are free to access. No paywall, no limits on viewing.

How It Works

1
Search or browse

Find YouTube videos through search, topic feeds, or the latest summaries list.

2
Get the summary

PicoBrief generates 5 summary formats: timestamps, key points, detailed notes, audio brief, and quiz.

3
Read, listen, or quiz

Switch between formats. Listen to the audio summary, read the notes, or test yourself with the quiz.

Information We Collect

Third-Party Services

Your Rights
